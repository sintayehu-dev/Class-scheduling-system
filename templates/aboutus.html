About FCSE | Class Scheduling System

This system was developed as part of our final project, with the primary goal of addressing the unique scheduling challenges faced by our faculty. By leveraging advanced algorithms and user-friendly interfaces, we strive to provide a robust and reliable solution for managing academic schedules, ensuring optimal use of resources and conflict-free timetables.

How does it Work?

Class Scheduling System makes use of a Genetic Algorithm that helps generate the most optimal and suited timetable to avoid different conflict and improve effective efficiency in preparing class schedule time table.
